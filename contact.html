Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all products. If you're not satisfied, simply return the item in original condition for a full refund.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping is available for urgent orders and takes 1-2 business days.

Do you offer customization?

Yes! We offer customization options for select products. Contact our team for details and pricing.

Are your products genuine leather?

Absolutely! All our products are made from 100% genuine leather sourced from trusted suppliers. Each product comes with an authenticity certificate.

How do I care for my leather products?

We provide detailed care instructions with every purchase. Generally, clean with a soft cloth and use leather conditioner periodically.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship within India. We're expanding to international markets soon. Follow us for updates!
